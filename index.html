    ABOUT

    Founded as the "School of International Dance" by Christina Heimlich in 1962, International Dance, LLC is now owned, operated, and directed by former Christina Heimlich student, Elizabeth Hawkins.

    Miss Elizabeth, Assistant Director Rebecca Collier, and our amazing instructors and staff continue the traditions and standards set forth by our founder.

    International Dance accepts students from the age of 3 (potty trained) to adult and offers pre-ballet, ballet, adult ballet, tap, adult tap, jazz, lyrical, modern, pointe, creative movement, as well as international folk, and character dance.

    International Dance instructors encourage development of motor coordination skills, balance, partnering, flexibility, strength, concentration, independence, creativity through imagination, poise, posture, rhythm, discipline, and well-being - all while having fun, and learning about music and dance.
