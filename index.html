    Welcome to International Dance!

    Inspiring generations of dancers since 1962 through award winning passion and excellence.

    ABOUT

    Founded as the "School of International Dance" by Christina Heimlich in 1962, International Dance, LLC. is now owned, operated, and directed by former Christina Heimlich student, Elizabeth Hawkins.

    Miss Elizabeth, Assistant Director Rebecca Collier, instructors and staff carry on the traditions that uphold the standards of our founder.

    International Dance accepts students of the age of 3 (potty trained) to adult and offers pre-ballet, ballet, adult ballet, tap, adult tap, jazz, lyrical, modern, pointe, creative movement, as well as international folk and character dance

    All International Dance classes encourage development of motor coordination skills, balance, partnering, flexibility, strength, concentration, independence, creativity through use of imagination, poise, posture, rhythm, discipline, and wellbeing - all while having fun and learning about music and dance.
