    Welcome to

    Founded in 1962 by world-renown international star Christina Heimlich, the School of International Dance has inspired dancers for generations through award winning passion and excellence.

    Today, Mrs. Heimlich's legacy and mantle are taken to new heights by Christina Heimlich Dancer alumna, Shenandoah University graduate, and the current International Dance owner, CEO, and Director Elizabeth (Banks) Hawkins.

    The thriving Alexandria (soon to be Annandale) VA premier school of dance offers classical ballet, tap, jazz, modern, lyrical, folk, and character dance instruction year-round for all ages.
